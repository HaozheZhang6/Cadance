## Sprocket Tooth Count Ranges

Source: Commercial sprocket catalogs (Tsubaki, Renold, SDP/SI)
Reference: https://www.sdp-si.com/resources/chain-sprocket-design.php

| Application | Tooth count z | Notes |
|-------------|--------------|-------|
| Minimum practical | 9 | Severe polygon effect below 9 |
| Small/fast sprockets | 9–17 | Driver sprockets, high speed |
| Medium sprockets | 17–38 | Most common range |
| Large/slow sprockets | 38–80 | Driven/idler, low speed |
| Maximum practical | 120 | Above 80 rarely stocked |
