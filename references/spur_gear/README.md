# ISO 54 / DIN 780 — Spur Gear Modules

## Standards
- **ISO 54:1996** — Cylindrical gears for general engineering and heavy engineering — Modules
- **DIN 780:1977** — Modules for cylindrical gears (superseded by ISO 54 but widely cited)

## What It Covers
Defines the preferred series of gear module values (m) for involute spur and helical gears.
Module m = pitch diameter / number of teeth = 1 / diametral pitch.
All other gear dimensions derive from m and z (tooth count).

### Key Formulas
- Pitch radius: r_p = m × z / 2
- Addendum (tip): r_a = r_p + m → Da = m × (z + 2)
- Dedendum (root): r_d = r_p − 1.25 × m
- Base circle: r_b = r_p × cos(α), α = 20° (standard pressure angle per ISO 53)
- Face width typical: 8 × m to 16 × m

## Scope Parameters
- Module range: 0.1 – 50 mm (ISO 54 full range)
- For mechanical CAD: 0.5 – 20 mm most common
- Standard pressure angle: 20° (ISO 53); 14.5° legacy
- Tooth count: practical minimum z = 12 (to avoid undercut at 20°); typically 12–200
