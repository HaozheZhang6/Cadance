# DIN 2573 / EN 1092-1 — Slip-On Flanges, PN 6

## Standards
- **DIN 2573:1998** — Flanges; slip-on welding flanges, PN 6 (nominal pressure 6 bar)
- **EN 1092-1:2018** — Flanges and their joints — Circular flanges for pipes, valves, fittings and accessories (Type 01 = flat plate / slip-on, PN 6)
- Note: DIN 2573 has been largely harmonized with EN 1092-1 PN 6. Both specify the same dimensional series.

## What It Covers
Circular flanges welded onto pipe ends. Defines outer diameter (D), bolt circle diameter (K), number of bolts (n), bolt hole diameter (d), and flange thickness (b) for each nominal bore DN.

Mating standard: DIN 2527 (blank flanges), DIN 2576 (weld neck flanges, PN 10).
Bolt standard: ISO 4014 hex bolts + ISO 4032 hex nuts.

## Pressure Rating
PN 6 = 6 bar (0.6 MPa) maximum working pressure at 20°C.
Higher pressures: PN 10 (DIN 2576), PN 16 (DIN 2527), PN 25, PN 40.

## Scope Parameters
- DN range: DN 10 – DN 600 (nominal bore in mm)
- Outer diameter D: 75 mm (DN10) to 715 mm (DN600)
- Bolt count: 4 (DN10–DN80), 8 (DN100–DN200), 12 (DN250–DN300), up to 24 (DN600)
- Bolt sizes: M10 – M24 (per DN range)
