Create a pillow block bearing housing. Base plate: 100mm x 50mm x 12mm with 4 mounting holes (8mm diameter) at corners, 10mm from edges. Central bearing bore: 35mm diameter through the full height. Add a cylindrical boss (50mm outer diameter, 30mm tall) centered on top of the base plate surrounding the bearing bore. The top of the boss should have a 2mm x 45° chamfer on the outer edge. Add an oil groove: 3mm wide, 2mm deep channel around the inside of the bore at mid-height of the boss.
